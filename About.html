About Critch Streetwear



The first physcial store ever was on Downtown Mcallen, Texas

Critch Streetwear founder Christian Valles hails from Manila, Philippines, ever since he was a teenager he always had a certain passion for clothing and selling. This entrepreneuerial mindset stems from his dreams to be financially stable at a young age, while attending college in the Philippines he also had an online clothing store where he sold thrifted and manufactured clothes alike.



Christian moved to texas back on december 2022 and despite all the hardships that come from adjusting to a new faraway place, there was one thing that remained familiar in his life, that was his passion for clothes. The first store was also an online store focusing on streetwear and vintage brands, he started selling on mercari, depop, stock X and ebay, after gaining traction he started his first physical store locally on Mcallen, Texas



Until now Critch's is still striving to bring the best of the best to the customers, staying true to our roots of giving the best deals ever in the market, our number one goal is to make the customers proud of what they wear and to have the best value for their money.
